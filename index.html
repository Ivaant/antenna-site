We locate and relocate talents

In Antenna Recruiting,
we have our solution to a problem of lack of high qualified
technical specialists and engineers in Germany.

Source in Easter Europe

We locate young, ambitious, high qualified technical talents in eastern Europe and with great care relocate them to Germany.

Qualified and multilingual

Technical education was always a strong side in eastern Europe. Our candidates speak one ore more foreign languages and open for opportunities outside of their countries.

Talented and ambitious

We select the best candidates meeting your needs. It could be young university graduate or experienced candidate. Together we bring more life and dynamic into your business.

Integration is a key

We don’t stop at employment. We offer also integration program for successful relocation.

How it works

1

Send us your vacancy and we come back to you with a candidate.

2

We make an appointment for a video call with candidate of your choice

3

As our main source base is in Ukraine it is easy, fast and not expensive to invite a candidate for a personal interview in your plant (we have low-cost flight connections and no visa is required for a visit)

4

As our candidates are highly qualified technical specialists they become so called “blue card”, and in case of successful job offer it takes usually 2 weeks till job permission is obtained.

5

We know what is needed for successful integration into work and social community. On your wish we offer our Integration program where we coach and support candidates to make relocation a success.
